Versace Pour Homme

Aroma : wine glass, woody.

Initial note : bergamot, neroli, bitter orange leaf, lemon peel.

Final note : musk, ambergris, sycamore wood.

This time the representatives of the perfume arts are looking for the keys to the unpredictable and ambiguous female nature. She has always been and remains a mystery. Workers of the famous Lacoste brand perfume shop find ways to express the beautiful ladies' inner world again with sophisticated compositions.
